## How to use it:

1.  **Select your group** (e.g., "Home 2026") from the sidebar.
2.  Look for the **"+ Add Member"** button next to the group name at the top.
3.  Click it to open the add member form.
4.  **Select a friend** from the dropdown list.
5.  Click **"Add"**.

## Result:
*   The new member is instantly added to the group.
*   They will **automatically appear** in the **"Who paid?"** dropdown when you add a new expense.
*   They will also be available for splitting expenses.

## Note:
*   The person must be in your **Friends list** first. If not, add them using the "+" button in the Friends sidebar section first.
